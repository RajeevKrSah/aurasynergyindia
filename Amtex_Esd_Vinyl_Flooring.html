Amtex ESD Vinyl Flooring

Amtex ESD Vinyl Flooring

AURA AMTEX Range ESD vinyl flooring, which is also known as antistatic flooring is designed to control static electricity in environments sensitive to electrostatic discharge (ESD). It helps protect electronic equipment and components from potential damage caused by static electricity. The flooring material typically contains conductive elements that dissipate static charges, providing a controlled path for them to discharge safely. This is crucial in industries like Hospitals, Critical Care Units, electronics manufacturing etc where static-sensitive devices are handled.

Application

It is simple to repair and easy to clean The joints between individual tiles is welded by heat fusion process to get a seamless floor the joints in the flooring and is sealed by using a PVC welding bar of matching colour to supply by the manufacturer using a hot air gun for fusion of welding bar with flooring.

Installation

The flooring would be installed on a smooth, clean sub floor which should be free from any undulation Copper grounding strips will be laid flat on the floor in the conductive adhesive and connect to copper wire of grounding.

Design Options

AURA AMTEX ESD vinyl flooring often comes in various designs and colors, allowing hospitals to choose options that match their aesthetic preferences while maintaining functionality.

When implementing SVT conductive flooring in OT areas, consulting with professionals familiar with healthcare facility regulations and guidelines is advisable to ensure proper installation and compliance.

When considering ESD vinyl flooring for hospitals, it's essential to ensure that it meets industry standards and regulations for healthcare environments.
